# About MITM.life

MITM.life is a premier platform dedicated to Open Source Intelligence (OSINT) and cybersecurity research. Our mission is to provide organizations and security professionals with the intelligence and tools they need to stay ahead of emerging threats.

## Our Mission

We believe that knowledge is the first line of defense. By providing comprehensive OSINT services and sharing cutting-edge research, we help our clients make informed decisions about their cybersecurity posture.

- **Threat Intelligence Analysis**
- **Cybersecurity Research**
